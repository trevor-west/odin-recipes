Grandma's Gingersnaps

Grandma's delicious gingersnap cookies

This is a recipe for a delicious gingersnap cookie that was passed down by someone's grandma.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups white sugar, divided
  • ¾ cup margarine
  • 1 large egg
  • ¼ cup molasses
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 tablespoon ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon salt
